Why is it important to measure Happyness at Work?

Understanding what really drives employees is not a choice anymore. It has become an imperative. With today’s workforce, measuring engagement levels isn’t enough. By measuring happyness at work, organizations can make better decisions, launch more accurate initiatives and drive accountability. This is what will give organizations to stay ahead of the curve and retain the right talent to excel!

What is the Happyness Quotient TM tool all about?

Happyness Quotient TM is a cloud based tool powered by Never Grow Up ®. It attempts to understand what makes an individual happy in life and more specifically, at work. It’s a tool that is fun to administer, customizable and comprehensive in nature.

How much access to the results of the tool will my employees get? Is giving employees an access to the data necessary?

We believe it’s the HR Team in your organization that should guide us on this one. When employees are asked a series of work life related questions, they are bound to be curious about the results. Giving them access to the data makes the process a transparent one. So, based on your company policies, culture and values, together we can decide how much access to give and we can customise reports accordingly.

How will I know if my employees are being honest while answering the tool?

Every question of the tool has been created making sure it is not biased and suggestive. Predominantly, every question has been made into a situation that the employee is likely to face at work. Since happiness is a subjective construct, understanding what one would do in different situations is the most reliable way of understanding an employee’s mind-set.

14

How can I get my employees to participate and answer all the questions of the tool?

The good thing about Happyness Quotient TM is that the questions can be staggered and sent to the employees over a period of time. There are two ways in which we can ensure employees participate and answer all the questions.

1) Are questions are fun and creative. We are quite sure your employees will look forward to answering the questions as it won’t feel like the ‘typical’ survey they have been shown before.

2) Unless a question sent earlier isn’t answered, the system doesn’t allow the employee to answer the other questions of the tool. This also helps ensure every question is being answered.

How can I make sure this does not distract them from their work?

How many questions get asked at one time is decided by you. Tell us what suits your employees best and we can configure the back-end operations of the tool accordingly.
